How It Works

Zero Trust.
Total Control.

Every step in the SecureVault pipeline eliminates assumptions and enforces mathematical verification at every layer.

01
Generate Identity
Register with email, phone, and password. Your phone number converts to a unique cryptographic HashID — your true immutable identity.
02
Two-Factor Authentication
Sign in using HashID (Step 1) then password (Step 2). Both layers must pass independently before vault access is granted.
03
Upload & Encrypt
Choose any file, select AES, RSA, or DES, enter your key. The vault locks it mathematically — unreadable without your key.
04
Decrypt on Demand
Upload the encrypted file, provide the correct key. The engine verifies, decrypts, and returns your original data in-browser.

Algorithm Engine

Three Layers.
One Vault.

AES uses Fernet symmetric encryption — the industry standard. RSA and DES provide asymmetric and legacy layers. Choose the algorithm that fits your threat model.

— 01
🔐
AES
256-Bit · Fernet Symmetric
Industry standard encryption. Fernet guarantees authenticity and confidentiality. Best for all general-purpose file encryption — fast, strong, provably secure.
— 02
🧠
RSA
2048-Bit · Asymmetric
Public-key cryptography for secure key exchange. Ideal for scenarios requiring different keys for encryption and decryption — the foundation of modern PKI.
— 03
⚡
DES
Legacy · Demonstration
Classic 56-bit block cipher. Provided for legacy compatibility and educational demonstration of symmetric encryption fundamentals and historical context.
